This directory will likely be empty. Until you add files to it,
your panoramas will not display.

Add these files from your Flash Panorama Player distribution to this directory
on your webserver:

- menuFullscreen.swf
- autorotator.swf
- glassMeter.swf
- movDecoder.swf
- show_pano.swf
- pano.swf

If you haven't purchased Flash Panorama Player, go to
	http://flashpanoramas.com/player/
